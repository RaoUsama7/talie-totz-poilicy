When you use the Service, we may collect the following personal information:

  • Profile: After registration, a profile page ("Profile") will be created for you.
  • Speaking Activities: Some activities involve speaking. Your audio may be sent to a third-party provider.
  • Activity Data & IP Addresses: We may generate data about your use of the Service.
  • Google Analytics: We use Google Analytics to understand how users engage with our website.
  • Language Preferences: We collect information about the languages parents speak.
  • Usage Data: We track how long users spend on specific pages or activities.
  • Device Information: We collect information about the devices used to access the app.
  • Login Information: We collect data about how often users log in.
  • Activity Metrics: We collect usage metrics to understand user engagement.
